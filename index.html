About Resala AUC

Empowering students to make a difference in their community

Resala AUC stands as a beacon of hope and service within the American University in Cairo community. Our mission transcends traditional student activities, focusing on creating meaningful change through dedicated volunteer work and community engagement.

We believe that every individual has the power to contribute to society, and through our organized efforts, we channel this potential into impactful actions that benefit those in need.
